The story of Genord Motors


Genord Motors began not in a garage or boardroom, but in an experimental biology lab - where unconventional thinking met mechanical ambition. Founded in 2018 by a small team of engineers and researchers with backgrounds in automotive design and bio-electric sciences, Genord was created with one radical goal: to build the first car powered by ideas no one else would touch.

In the early days, our prototypes were as much scientific experiments as they were vehicles. We explored energy sources that traditional automakers had long dismissed - drawing inspiration from biological systems, novel materials, and strange, brilliant efficiencies discovered through bold, iterative experimentation. Our first major breakthrough came in 2021, when a test system originally intended for micro-scale efficiency studies evolved into the propulsion core of what would become the Model F.

While our methods have occasionally drawn scrutiny, our results have been undeniable. Genord has led the way in reducing charging frequency, eliminating traditional exhaust systems, and pioneering lightweight but incredibly strong chassis materials. Today, we stand as one of the most unpredictable and forward-thinking names in clean mobility - not just building cars, but challenging the industry to think bolder, weirder, and better.
