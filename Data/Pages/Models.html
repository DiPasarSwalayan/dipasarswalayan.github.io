MODELS

We have a wide variety of models fit for royalty while remaining accessible to the common pleb.

Model F

The Model F represents the future of sustainable transportation - an all-electric, zero-emissions vehicle that blends cutting-edge technology with elegant design. Engineered for maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact, this model offers a smooth, silent ride and a long-range battery that redefines what electric mobility can be.

Model U

Model U is our high-performance luxury supercar, built for those who demand both style and speed. With a sleek aerodynamic frame, a powerful engine, and precision handling, it delivers a driving experience that's nothing short of thrilling. Every detail, from the handcrafted interior to the state-of-the-art infotainment system, speaks to sophistication and innovation.

Model C

Model C is a curious creation - equal parts quirky and practical. It doesn't follow trends or stick to convention, but that's exactly its charm. Designed with an unpredictable aesthetic and a feature set that's as eclectic as its inspiration, this model is for those who love a car with character and aren't afraid to stand out.

Model K

The Model K is where our journey began - handcrafted in a humble garage and built with vision, passion, and limited tools. Though simple in design, it holds a special place in our history as the original blueprint that sparked an entire lineup. It's a reminder of how far we've come and what determination can create.
